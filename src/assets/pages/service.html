How Cosmic Explorer Works

Cosmic Explorer is designed to make your exploration of the universe seamless and enjoyable. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter a search term related to space or celestial bodies in the search bar.
  2. Click the search button, and Cosmic Explorer will fetch relevant information from NASA's Open API.
  3. Explore the results, which include fascinating facts, stunning images, and intriguing details about your query.

Start your cosmic journey today!
